5 EASY HONEY RECIPES

Sweet, wholesome ideas to brighten your day.

Honey isn’t just delicious — it’s also packed with antioxidants and natural healing properties. Here are five healthy recipes that let you enjoy the sweetness of honey while keeping things light and nutritious.

Greek Yogurt with Honey & Nuts

Ingredients: 

 1 cup of Greek yogurt 

 1 tablespoon of honey 

 1/4 cup of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews, etc.)

 A pinch of cinnamon (optional) 

 

 Instructions: 

 Place the Greek yogurt in a bowl. 

 Drizzle the honey over the yogurt. Top with mixed nuts for crunch and texture. 

 Optional: Sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon for extra flavor.

 Enjoy immediately as a healthy snack or breakfast.

Honey Lemon Detox Drink

Ingredients: 

 1 cup of warm water

 1 tablespoon of honey 

 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice 

 A pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)

 

 Instructions: 

 Heat a cup of water until warm (not boiling). 

 Stir in honey and lemon juice. 

 Optionally, add a pinch of cayenne pepper for extra kick. 

 Stir well and drink it in the morning to kick-start your day or before meals for digestion support.

Fresh Fruit Salad with Honey Drizzle

Ingredients: 

1 apple, chopped 1 

banana, sliced 1/2

 cup of grapes, halved 1/2

 orange, peeled and cut into segments

 1 tablespoon of honey

 1 tablespoon of lemon juice (optional)

 

 Instructions: 

Combine all the chopped fruits in a large bowl.

 Drizzle the honey over the fruit mixture.

 Optionally, add lemon juice for an extra zing.

 Toss gently to combine and serve immediately.

1 slice of whole wheat bread
