types of honey

unique flavors and benefits of each honey variety.

Honey is a natural product with a rich variety of flavors, colors, and health benefits. These differences come from the types of flowers that bees collect nectar from. Each type of honey offers something unique — from mild and sweet to dark and strong — and can serve different purposes, whether for boosting immunity, soothing a sore throat, or simply adding depth to your meals.

Manuka Honey

Origin: New Zealand and Australia 

Benefits: Strong antibacterial properties,wound healing  

Taste: Earthy and slightly bitter

Clover Honey

Origin: North America 

Benefits: Mild antioxidant effects, ideal for everyday use 

Taste: Light, sweet, and floral

Acacia Honey

Origin: Europe and North America 

Benefits: High in antioxidants, low glycemic index 

Taste: Very mild and sweet, almost vanilla-like

Buckwheat Honey

Origin: United States and Eastern Europe 

Benefits: Rich in iron and antioxidants, helpful for cough relief 

Taste: Strong, dark, and malty

Orange Blossom Honey

Origin: Citrus-growing regions like Florida and Mexico 

Benefits: Light calming properties, contains vitamins 

Taste: Light and citrusy
